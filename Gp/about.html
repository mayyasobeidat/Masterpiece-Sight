FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a photoshoot on Sight?
You can book a photoshoot through our platform by browsing the available options, selecting a photographer, and completing the booking process.
Can clients book a photo session with a specific photographer?
Yes, clients can book a photo session with a specific photographer based on the type of photography they offer or based on the city where the photographer is located.
What types of photography services does the site offer?
The site offers a variety of photography services including :
  • portrait photography
  • food photography
  • landscape photography
  • newborn photography
  • product photography
  • occasions photography
Can I preview the photographer's work before booking a photoshoot?
Yes, you can view the photographer's portfolio on their profile before booking a photoshoot to get a sense of their style and the quality of their work.
Is there a customer support team available?
Yes, our customer support team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns. You can reach us by email or through the contact form on our website.
Does Sight offer design services?
A: Currently, Sight does not offer design services. However, we are working towards adding these services in the near future.
Does Sight offer video photography services?
Some of our photographers have the skill of video photography, you can inquire about this service with each photographer individually or by checking their profile to see if video photography is one of their skills.
